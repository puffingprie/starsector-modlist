Location: Outer reaches of the Taivassija system
Date: 193.09.25

You were an ordinary fleet officer, leading your cruiser Noman through several missions in various detachments, and your fleet merits were accumulating like any other ordinary officer.

Until one day, you hear of the 3rd Fire of Desire detachment disappearing from your transponder after going into a black hole system. Soon after, the detachment you were in was struck by a Hegemony lightning strike, the flagship destroyed, and a mixture of severed limbs and molten steel solidified on your ship.

But you are truly the darling of fate.

The detachment's sword was broken, your Noman became a survivor, and you even became acting captain. Gathering the men still left to patch themselves up and prepare for further withdrawal.

Your strategic skills proved to be rather lacking...
