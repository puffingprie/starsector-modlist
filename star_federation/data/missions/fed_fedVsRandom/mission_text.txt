Location: Somewhere in the Mediosolar Expanse
Date: Unknown

Command a randomly Star Federation faction fleet and battle a random equally matched other faction. Factions cannot fight themselves.
Features an improved battlespace terrain generator and fleet balance.

Click on the mission in the list to re-roll. While clicking:
If you hold Ctrl D: Disable enemy FP advantage.
If you hold Ctrl S: Allow modded factions and Federation infighting.
If you hold Ctrl R: Enforces station presence in battles (No indicator otherwise).
