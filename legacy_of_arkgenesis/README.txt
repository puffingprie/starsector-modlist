Legacy of Arkgneisis requires the libraries LazyLib and GraphicsLib and magicLib.
http://fractalsoftworks.com/forum/index.php?topic=5444.0
http://fractalsoftworks.com/forum/index.php?topic=10982
http://fractalsoftworks.com/forum/index.php?topic=13718.0

If you experience graphics artifacts or unacceptable levels of slowdown, go to the GraphicsLib mod folder and edit the GRAPHICS_OPTIONS.ini to disable or lower the shader effects. Most of the high-end graphics options can be turned off or lowered in quality.
